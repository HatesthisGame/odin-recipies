Haluski

Haluski is a Polish classic. This vegan take keeps all the wonderful taste of the orginal with less of the impact. You will substitute orzo for the standard egg noodles. The small little bights of cabbage and orzo will blow ya mind baby. This buttery ass dinner will rock your shit.

Haluski

What You'll need

  • 1/2box orzo
  • 6 tablespoons butter, unsalted
  • 1 large onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 green cabbage
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Boil orzo according to instructions on box.
  2. Shred cabbage, onion, garlic. The smaller the better. We tryna go small here boi.melt butter and add ingredients
  3. Add orzo, combine, salt and pepper to taste, serve.
